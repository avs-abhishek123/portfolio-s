About Shreyash

Mr. Shreyash Kawlakar is a a visionary AI innovator, orchestrating an industry transformation through his trailblazing expertise. With a role as Principal Architect at Datadecode.ai Pvt Ltd, he's redefining product growth dynamics with groundbreaking solutions like Fusion AI®, empowering businesses to thrive while he refines workflows. As a master of Python scripting and algorithmic development, Shreyash optimizes data analytics, bolstering remarkable efficiency gains in his leadership of Intelligent Hyper Automation®. From spearheading Driverless AI® to global collaborations on revolutionary applications like "Formulation AI," he artfully converges cutting-edge technology with tangible impact. This amalgamation of pioneering spirit, technical prowess, and collaborative vision makes Shreyash a driving force in shaping the future of AI-powered industries.

Why we do it

We believe in making satellite data accessible to everyone – to help people understand how to make the world a better place. By combining satellite data with data gathered on Earth and presenting them on ORBIS, Astrosat can deliver actionable intelligence that helps to solve socio-economic, societal and environmental problems faced around the world.
